💡 Coding Challenge: 
Write a C program that generates a password using an email and phone number.  

📌 Requirements:
  
🔹 Take 2 inputs from the user:  
       - Email (max 50 characters)  
       - Phone number (max 10 characters)  

🔹 The password should be:  
       - First 4 characters of the email ✉  
       - First 5 digits of the phone number 📞  

🔹 Use *fgets()* for input safety (avoid scanf()).  
🔹 Use *string.h* functions (strncpy, strncat).  

📌 Example:  
👉 Input:  

Enter the email: oysterkode@gmail.com  
Enter the phone number: 9876543210  

👉 Output:  

Your password is: oyst98765
